Horse Family Simulator

Description

Horse Family Simulator is a Unity-based animal simulation game where the player takes control of a horse and experiences life from a horse's perspective. Players can explore the environment, interact with other animals, complete tasks, and participate in activities such as grazing, running, jumping, and family bonding.

The game leverages Malbers Animal Controller to provide realistic horse movement, animation blending, and natural behaviors. Players experience dynamic interactions with the environment and NPC animals, enhancing immersion and realism.

This project demonstrates expertise in character controller integration, 3D environment design, animation handling, and interactive gameplay mechanics in Unity. It also emphasizes smooth camera control and responsive input for a highly engaging player experience.
